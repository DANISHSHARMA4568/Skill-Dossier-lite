About SkillDossier

Connecting mentors and mentees for meaningful career growth.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality mentorship. SkillDossier connects ambitious learners with experienced professionals, creating meaningful relationships that accelerate career growth and skill development.

Safety & Guidelines

For Mentees

  • Be respectful of your mentor's time and expertise
  • Come prepared with specific questions and goals
  • Maintain professional communication at all times
  • Follow through on commitments and action items

For Mentors

  • Set clear boundaries and expectations upfront
  • Provide constructive and actionable feedback
  • Respect confidentiality and privacy
  • Foster a supportive learning environment
